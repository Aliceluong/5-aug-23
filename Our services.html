  • Crown and bridges

    • At DC Smile Clinic, we believe in enhancing your smile with precision and grace. Our Crown and Bridges treatments are specially tailored to restore functionality and aesthetics to your teeth. A crown, often referred to as a "cap," is an ideal solution for a damaged or decayed tooth, encapsulating the afflicted area and rejuvenating its appearance. Each crown is meticulously crafted to match the color, shape, and alignment of your teeth, providing a natural look that radiates confidence. Bridges, on the other hand, work to fill gaps left by missing teeth. Supported by crowns on adjacent teeth or implants, bridges are custom-designed to blend seamlessly with your existing smile. Our highly skilled dental professionals utilize the latest technologies and materials to ensure that every bridge is strong, durable, and visually harmonious.
